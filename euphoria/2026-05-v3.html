Evidence-Tiered Closure — 14 Quantifiable Levers

The May 2026 plan above runs on top of a foundation that must hold. We re-ran this report against the Cannabis-Specialist SEO Operating Catalog (22 tracking domains · 50-item evidence-weighted priority order · Whitespark 2026 LSRF · BrightLocal 2024-25 · Citation Building Group · Cannabis Creative + CannaPlanners). 14 quantifiable levers were identified the original plan did not address — including two cannabis-specific binary checks that can each be the single biggest rank mover when broken, and a NAP audit scope expansion that builds directly on Pillar 1's audit fix.

Cannabis-specific hot-takes (apply to Euphoria).
  • Default Dutchie iframe is a critical bug, not a feature. Pillar 1 caught the San Diego footer NAP issue — but if euphoriawellness.com/menu is iframe-loaded, every product URL is invisible to Google regardless of NAP integrity. Cannabis Creative + CannaPlanners consensus: this is the single highest-leverage cannabis-specific SEO fix available.
  • The age-gate is a stealth indexation killer. If implemented client-side without a Googlebot allowlist, the homepage Google crawls is the empty age-verification page — not "first licensed dispensary in Las Vegas" content. Verify in server logs.
  • NAP exact-match across all properties drives a documented +23% local-pack rank lift (Citation Building Group). Pillar 1 audited the website footer — Foundation extends to Weedmaps, Leafly, Yelp, BBB, Bing, Apple Maps, Foursquare, and 5+ NV cannabis directories.
