DeadlineEOD May 7
Time2 hrs

Steps

  1. In GSC, run URL Inspection on https://euphoriawellnessnv.com/. Click "Test live URL" → after the test runs, click "View tested page" → switch to the HTML tab.

5-question FAQ (paste)
How far is Euphoria Wellness from Harry Reid International Airport?
About 3 minutes from Terminal 1 and 6 minutes from Terminal 3 — straight up I-15. Most rideshares quote $12–18 one-way.

Can I bring cannabis purchases on my flight?
No. Cannabis is federally prohibited from flights and from being transported across state lines, even between two recreational states. Consume in Nevada or have us deliver to your hotel where allowed.

Where can I legally consume what I buy?
Nevada law restricts cannabis consumption to private 21+ residences and licensed consumption lounges. Hotels have their own rules — always check with the property. Public smoking, driving impaired, and consumption in casinos are illegal.

Do I need a Nevada ID?
No. Any valid government-issued photo ID showing you're 21+ is accepted (REAL ID, passport, or out-of-state driver's license).

Does Euphoria deliver to Strip hotels?
Yes — to the concierge desks of most cooperating Strip hotels. Hotel policies vary; we'll confirm at the time of order whether delivery to your specific hotel is allowed.
