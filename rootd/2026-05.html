1c. Verify the age-gate doesn't block crawlers

The April 27 audit flagged this as a critical risk. If the age verification gate blocks Googlebot (e.g., the homepage content only renders post-confirmation), every signal we add downstream is invisible to Google. Verification is a single Search Console / live-test check; fix is a server-side allow-list for known bot user agents.

1d. Remove the "Offers delivery" GBP attribute

Root'd does not offer delivery, but the GBP currently has "Offers delivery" enabled under Service Options. This creates a misleading signal that conflicts with reality — Google has been known to demote listings whose attributes don't match observed user behavior. Cost to fix: 30 seconds in the GBP dashboard. Expected effect: cleaner relevance signal across all 7 keywords.

Low

Mitigation (per Q1 consensus): Layer SMS opt-in incentive ON TOP of the review discount, don't replace. Review velocity is too important to compromise. Different prompts, different moments — review at receipt, SMS at POS entry.
